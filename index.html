FAQs

Do you ever ask for seed phrases or private keys?

Never. Connections are read-only. We’ll only ask for standard wallet permissions to view balances for alert personalisation.

What blockchains are supported?

We’re starting with majors (e.g., Ethereum and Solana) and expanding based on demand.

How do rewards work?

Post useful alpha, verify news, or flag scams to earn points. Points convert to $GUARD periodically; higher-quality contributions earn more.

What makes verification trustworthy?

A layered model: dev confirmations, Crypto Lifeguard team checks, and token-backed community consensus. Items with strongest alignment surface first.
